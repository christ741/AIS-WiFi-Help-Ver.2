    How to log in:

    Access your AIS Student account

    Enter your assigned username into the username box and enter your assigned password into the password box, then press login to access the internet.

    Don't have an assigned username or password? consult Mr. Andrew Chiu or the office administration.

    Forgot your password? Reset password?

    Click here and enter your email address

    Go to your AIS gmail account and you will receive a new password
